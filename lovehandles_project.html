FAST AND FURIOUS

with a dash of love (handles)

This is Lovehandles. Meet him (her?). Love him. Cherish him. Because that right there was the product of months of sweat, tears, and well, love.

Packed in that small(ish) exterior is quite a powerhouse of 36V of battery, a ___ motor, and enough power to max out at a solid 20 mph with a medium-female-sized rider.

We were posed with a few design constraints:our car could have no more than four 12V batteries, it could not exceed 20 mph (for safety reasons), it needed to be able to carry a single rider, and we needed to stay within a budget of $400. With these parameters in mind, we set out on our merry ways to conceptualize, design, and finally build what is now Lovehandles.

As you might expect with two novice go kart builders, Rachel and i hit many roadblocks along the way as we built our baby.
