AUTONOMOUS NAVIGATION BOT

Search algorithms and maze navigation

This autonomous robot was the final project for MIT's Introduction to Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Class (6.01). It was designed with optimized search algorithms to traverse a maze by itself. In order to optimize performance, the robot needed to be able to distinguish noise from actual wall detection. Using a Bayesian update process to update present and past states of the robot and sonar sensors to detect the walls, our robot was one of five to complete the full course of the maze.
