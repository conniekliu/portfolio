LASER CUT EARRINGS

Jewelery for the woodshop nerd

A side project of mine and a great intersection between engineering and art. With some scrap plywood I found in the Singapore University fab lab and the in-house laser cutter, I experimented with designing wooden earrings using Solidworks sketches and CorelDraw. Happy to share patterns if you're interested in laser cutting your own :)
